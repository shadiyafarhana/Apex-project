THE SCHOOL WITH INTERNATIONAL STANDARD

Apex International School is a secondary school affiliated to CBSE, Delhi, established in the year 2005. It is situated at Odumbra, in Olavanna Panchayath, Calicut. The school is located in a sprawling 3-acre green campus, 1.5 km from Mini Bye-Pass. It is a co-educational institution that caters to students from K.G to Std X. The mission of the school is to promote education that builds fundamental characters such as honesty, compassion, courage, persistence, and responsibility. The management envisages a completely different school with personalized care to develop intellectual and social skills.
